Title: Ilimex Poultry Trial – A.J. Forster (Crop 1) External Summary
Document Type: Trial Summary
Sector: Poultry
Use: External Bot
Last Updated: 2026

Overview:
A commercial poultry trial was conducted at A.J. Forster’s farm comparing a house fitted with the Ilimex Flufence system against a control house operating under standard conditions.

The objective was to isolate the impact of the Ilimex system by ensuring all other environmental and management variables remained consistent between the two houses.

Trial Design:
- House 2: Ilimex Flufence installed
- House 5: Control (no Ilimex system)
- Same breed, stocking density, and management practices
- Environmental conditions monitored using Hotraco Fortica systems
- Key variables tracked: CO₂, temperature, water consumption, ventilation, pressure

Environmental Consistency:
Environmental data across both houses showed closely aligned trends throughout the crop, confirming that performance differences are likely attributable to the Ilimex system rather than external factors.

Key Results:
- Reduction in total mortality in the Ilimex house compared to control
- Approximate mortality improvement of 0.5% across the crop
- Equivalent to over 100 fewer bird losses in the Ilimex house

Performance Insights:
- The Ilimex-treated house achieved its best historical performance
- Improvement of approximately 10p per bird compared to its own historical baseline
- Control house was historically one of the strongest-performing houses on the farm

- Positive impact observed in:
  - Bird liveability
  - Overall bird weight performance

Engineering Note:
During this initial trial, a ventilation-related issue was identified in the Ilimex house which affected internal air balance. This was subsequently addressed and is expected to improve performance consistency in future trials.

Conclusion:
The trial demonstrates encouraging early-stage results for the Ilimex Flufence system in commercial poultry production.

Despite operating against a high-performing control house, the Ilimex-treated house showed measurable improvements relative to its own historical performance, particularly in liveability and bird weight.

Further trials are ongoing to validate and build on these results under optimised conditions.

Approved Claims:
- Ilimex has demonstrated measurable reductions in poultry mortality under commercial conditions
- Early trials indicate improvements in bird performance and farm economics
- Results are based on controlled, side-by-side farm comparisons
- Continued trials are underway to further validate performance

Restricted Claims:
- Do not present results as guaranteed outcomes across all farms
- Do not disclose internal cost structures or margins
- Do not attribute all performance improvements solely to Ilimex without acknowledging broader farm variables
