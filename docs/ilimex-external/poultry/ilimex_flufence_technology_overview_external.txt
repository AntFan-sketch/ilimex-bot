Overview:
The Ilimex Flufence system is an air treatment and biosecurity solution designed to improve environmental conditions inside poultry houses by reducing airborne pathogen load and stabilising airflow quality.

It operates continuously alongside existing ventilation systems to enhance air hygiene without disrupting normal farm operations.

Core Function:
The system treats incoming and circulating air within the poultry house, targeting airborne pathogens and contaminants that can negatively impact bird health and performance.

By improving air quality, the system supports better flock liveability, performance consistency, and overall farm outcomes.

How It Works (Simplified):
1. Air Capture
Air is drawn into the Ilimex unit as part of the normal ventilation process.

2. Filtration Stage
Air passes through a specialised filtration layer designed to capture particulate matter, dust, and biological material that can carry pathogens.

3. UVC Treatment
The filtered air is exposed to high-intensity UVC light.

This process:
- Inactivates airborne bacteria, viruses, and fungal spores
- Reduces pathogen load within the airflow
- Limits the spread of disease through the environment

4. Clean Air Redistribution
The treated air is returned into the poultry house, contributing to a cleaner and more stable internal environment.

Integration with Existing Systems:
- Works alongside standard poultry ventilation systems
- Does not require major changes to farm infrastructure
- Designed for retrofitting onto existing houses or integration into new builds
- Compatible with climate control systems (e.g. Hotraco)

What It Targets:
- Airborne pathogens (bacteria, viruses, spores)
- Dust and particulate matter
- Environmental instability within the house

Why This Matters:
Air quality is a critical but often under-addressed factor in poultry performance.

Poor air quality can contribute to:
- Increased mortality
- Reduced weight gain
- Lower liveability
- Greater variability between crops

By improving air quality, Ilimex helps:
- Reduce mortality
- Improve bird performance
- Stabilise flock outcomes
- Enhance overall farm economics

Position in the Farm System:
The Ilimex Flufence system acts as a biosecurity layer within the ventilation system.

It complements:
- Existing ventilation controls
- Temperature management systems
- Standard hygiene and biosecurity practices

It does not replace these systems but enhances their effectiveness.

Operational Characteristics:
- Runs continuously during crop cycles
- Low operator intervention once installed
- Designed for commercial farm environments
- Built to operate under typical poultry house conditions

Evidence Basis:
The system has been tested in commercial poultry trials, including controlled side-by-side comparisons, demonstrating:
- Consistent reductions in mortality
- Improved flock performance relative to historical baselines
- Stable operation under real farm conditions

Differentiation:
Unlike standard filtration or ventilation systems alone, Ilimex combines:
- Mechanical filtration
- UVC pathogen inactivation
- Integration into existing airflow systems

This allows it to actively reduce airborne pathogen load rather than simply move or dilute contaminated air.
